INSTRUCTIONS FOR FILLING ONLINE APPLICATION FORM

The following steps are required to fill up the On-line application form: Before proceeding to fill-up the On-Line Application Form, the candidates are required to read the following instructions given below :


Step-1: Profile Registration Please use this step to give general information about you to register the profile. For profile registration, you need to have a valid email-id. In case, you donâ€™t have the same, please create an email-id before proceeding for profile registration. During profile registration, you need to set a password of length of 6 to 10 characters. No two online application forms can be filled with a single email-id; therefore if a candidate is willing to apply for two or more courses, he/she needs two or more email-ids so that for each course a separate online application form with a unique email-ID can be filled. The details of fields in the online application form have been illustrated in section-B. After entering the required information in On-line form, submit the form by clicking on SUBMIT button. A mail containing login information will be forwarded at your registered email-id. Please note down the admission form number carefully as you need to essentially mention it in all your future correspondence/queries related to your application form.


Step-2: Candidate Login Use your login id and the password to login again to proceed further for Application Form filling and proceed to Step-3.


Step-3: Fill Registration Form After entering the required information in the registration form, use edit Profile option to edit/correct your application (if needed) otherwise click on Proceed to Payment button. Make payment form shall be displayed on subsequent screen and click on Make Payment button to deposit the fee.


Step-4: Dispatch of Confirmation Page Please take the printout of the confirmation page of application form by affixing recent photograph (same as the one uploaded in application form), put signature and left-hand thumb impression of candidate and the signature of parent/guardian at appropriate places. The duly complete copy of the confirmation page of application formshould be sent along with copy of payment receipt to The Coordinator, Malaviya Entrance Test (MET), Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Deoria Road, Gorakhpur - 273010 (U.P.) so as to reach latest by 4 P.M. April 8, 2019. If the payment of the fee is made through online (e-payment) and due to some difficulties the candidate is not sure whether the payment is processed or not, then please use login tab to enter in your login account and use verify payment option to check your payment is successful or not. Please do keep 6 additional copies of the recent photograph (same as uploaded on ON-line application form) for future use.
